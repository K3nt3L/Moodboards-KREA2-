# 🎨 Krea 2 Moodboard Explorer

A web application designed to easily navigate a curated catalog of styles and mood boards optimized for **Krea 2**.

## ✨ Features

-   **Fast Navigation:** Intuitive browsing through a diverse collection of prompts.
-   **Visual Examples:** Preview different styles with illustrative thumbnails.
-   **Quick Access:** Instantly retrieve full prompt text and relevant keywords for your workflow.
